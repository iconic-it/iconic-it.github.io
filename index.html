üèÜ Best Campus Award ‚Äì ICTOB Season 1 (2022)

In 2022, Bangladesh Air Force Shaheen College Dhaka proudly received the Best Campus Award at the ICT Olympiad of Bangladesh (ICTOB) Season 1 ‚Äî a milestone achievement that highlighted our college's vibrant tech culture and student enthusiasm.

The award was granted based on participation count, and BAFSK made history with the highest number of recorded participants across all campuses in the country.

This outstanding turnout was made possible by the relentless efforts of the Iconic IT Club BAFSK, who hosted and managed the entire participant registration process within our campus. From organizing sign-ups to motivating students, our club played a pivotal role in mobilizing tech-minded talents to step into the national spotlight.

This moment stands as a proud legacy of how student-led initiatives can drive real impact and bring glory to our institution.

ICT Olympiad Season 2

üèÜ Back-to-Back Champions ‚Äì ICTOB Season 2 (2024‚Äì2025)

Following our historic achievement in Season 1, Bangladesh Air Force Shaheen College Dhaka has once again secured the prestigious Best Campus Ambassador Award in the ICT Olympiad of Bangladesh (ICTOB) Season 2, cementing our position as the leading institution in technology education and participation.

For the second consecutive time, our college recorded the highest number of participants nationwide, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to fostering technological excellence and digital literacy among students.

The Iconic IT Club BAFSK successfully organized and managed the entire registration process, mobilizing an even larger number of students compared to the previous season. Our coordinated efforts ensured maximum participation and engagement throughout the competition period.

The Season 2 journey culminated in an exhilarating Gala Round where our participants showcased exceptional talent and knowledge, with several students achieving remarkable positions in the national rankings.

This back-to-back achievement solidifies our legacy as pioneers in promoting ICT education and highlights the transformative impact of student-led initiatives in shaping the future of technology in Bangladesh.
